Learn about metabolism

01

What is metabolism?

Metabolism is a sum of chemical reactions that occur in the body, aiming to transform food into energy needed for cellular processes, convert food into "raw materials" for proteins, lipids, and more, and eliminate waste. Individuals with a fast metabolism use calories much faster than those with a slower metabolism. A great way to accelerate your metabolism is through regular exercise.

02

How does metabolism work?

Every time you eat, enzymes in the body's cells work together to transform food into energy that sustains the body's functions. Metabolism is like an engine of chemical reactions that keep the human body alive. However, the speed at which this engine operates varies from person to person.

03

What is metabolic rate?

Metabolic rate is the amount of chemical energy released by the body during an activity. This energy is measured in calories. Most often, kilocalories (kcal) are used to measure energy. Metabolic rate can be influenced by various factors such as diet, hormonal status, physical activity, or certain health issues.
